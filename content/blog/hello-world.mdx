this is the first post on my site. i've been meaning to start a blog for a while and kept putting it off for the usual reasons - not enough time, not sure what to write about, worried it wouldn't be good enough.

the thing is, i'm not writing this for an audience. i'm writing it to think.

## why a blog in 2026

writing forces clarity. you can hold a fuzzy idea in your head for weeks and feel like you understand it, and then the moment you try to write it down, you realize you don't.

i want to write about:
- software engineering - things i've learned, mistakes i've made, opinions i hold
- books - what i'm reading and what i think about it
- whatever else feels worth putting down

## how this site works

built with Next.js, Tailwind, and MDX. posts. simple, fast, easy to maintain.
